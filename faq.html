Cyber Arts Camp

  • When does the camp run?

    Cyber Arts Camp starts July 20th and will run for two weeks, Monday through Friday, for 10 total classes.

  • What is a day in cyber arts camp like?

    We start the day with an entertaining and educational video on the topic of the day. We then go through detailed instructions on how to build and work on the next piece in your sculpture. Expected time per day is 2 to 5 hours including video and building.

  • Will there be coaches or support?

    Yes! Our have Martian coaches that will help you along the way during studio hours and through online chat.

  • What will we be building?

    At the end of the camp, each student will have designed, built, soldered, and programmed their own unique light sculpture.

  • What results can a student expect?

    Students will walk away with a thorough understanding of physical computing, electronics, and a feeling of creative possibility and empowerment.

  • Are the materials provided?

    Yes, we will be sending a Martian kit that will have all the materials needed including LED Lights, Arduino, Soldering Kit, Solar Cell, Battery, Cables, and other Building Materials.

  • Is supervision advised?

    Adult supervision is advised during our soldering and solar power tutorials (1 day), as we'll be working with a soldering iron and electric wires.

  • Will computers be provided?

    No, students must have their own computer or laptop with internet access.
