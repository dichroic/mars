Meet Your Coaches

  • Ji

    Planet Earth

    Ji has been involved in building maker spaces combining high tech and off grid living in remote areas of the world. He is now doing the same on Mars. Join Ji as he meets various Martians, Humans, and other intergalactic creatures as he continues to learn about art, technology, and sustainability.

  • Alice

    Planet ?

    Our electronics sage has lead our team to build, fix, and repurpose hardware to build incredible tools. From solar-powered gadgets to kinetic toys give her a soldering iron and you'll be amazed at what she creates.

  • Alex

    Planet Disco

    We first discovered disco cat dancing under a disco ball out in a giant crater. We adopted Disco Cat who can do more than just play the best music and dance under the moonlight. It turns out Disco Cat has a knack for designing LED structures from small to big.

  • Kirby

    Planet Dikroos

    Hailing from the land of dichroic projections, Kirby's here to bring the rainbows to Mars. Geometry, Symmetry, and artistic creation, Bob Rossian she'll bring the sparkle to your terra-forms.

  • Anna

    Planet Disco

    Her planet is made out of mirrors allowing her to see into many dimensions. She brings this skill into her architecture creating spaces that are literally out of this world.

  • Gene

    Planet Pluto

    Born with a laptop already in hand programming has always come easy to Gene. He now uses artificial intelligence and robotics to help design everything from our rovers to our automated systems on Mars.
